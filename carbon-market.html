3. Market Analysis: Carbon Trading and Sustainability

Carbon Market Dynamics

Compliance vs Voluntary Markets

Compliance Market
  • UK Emissions Trading Scheme (ETS)
  • Government-regulated
  • Mandatory for certain industries
  • Allowance trading obligations
Voluntary Carbon Market (VCM)
  • Corporate self-imposed goals
  • High-integrity credits
  • Premium pricing for quality
  • Growing corporate adoption

Carbon Credit Quality Framework

Additionality

Reduction would not have occurred without the project

Permanence

Carbon remains stored for the credited period

Verification

Robust third-party auditing and monitoring

Leakage Prevention

No displacement of emissions to other locations
